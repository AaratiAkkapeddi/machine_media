Build a text generator

due Sep 20

Part I: talk to two chatbots:

Choose two chatbots from the list below (or find your own). Try having similar conversations with each. How do they respond? Submit a screenshot of your conversation.

  • Chat-GPT
  • jabberwacky
  • Eliza
  • Google Gemini
  • ReplikaM
  • 小冰
  • Bot A.L.I.C.E
  • Or any other chatbot you can find...

Part II: Finish corpus

Finish consolidating/creating found text to work with. It can be anything, but you will need to be able to explain where the text came from and why you chose it and in total, you will need at least 1000 words (the more the better). Put all of your text in one file.

link to demo code from class

Instructions for submission:

  • Submit screenshot from part I as an image file in the #assignments channel.
  • Submit your text corpus as a .txt file, .rtf file, or word document in the #assignments channel.
  • (optional) submit screenshots of your favorite generated sentences.
