Teachable Machine

due Sep 27

Read/Watch (Choose one):

  • Excavating A.I.
  • What does a dataset want?

Please come into class ready to talk about at least one aspect of the reading you chose that you found interesting.

Part I: Teachable Machine

Go to teachablemachine.withgoogle.com. Write 1-2 sentences about the kind of teachable machine model you'd like to create and why. Then using the steps we went over in class, create a simple image, audio, or pose model with two or more “classes” (categories). Save your model and paste the shareable link in the #assignments channel along with a sentence or two reflecting on the process.

To get shareable link:

1. Click export model

2. Click upload and then copy the link

3. (optional) Modify the p5 template from class to use your model. Feel free to continue to modify the p5 template as you want and to share with us the link to your p5.js sketch.

Instructions for submission:

  • Save your model and paste the shareable link in the #assignments channel along with your sentence or two reflecting on the idea for your model. (Optional: share your p5.js sketch if you continued to step 3)
